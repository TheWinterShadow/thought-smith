# Privacy Policy for Thought Smith

**Last updated: December 23, 2025**

## Introduction

Thought Smith ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Thought Smith (the "App"), available on Android and iOS.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly
- **Chat Messages**: The conversations you have with AI assistants within the app
- **AI API Keys**: The API keys you provide for OpenAI, Google Gemini, or Anthropic Claude services
- **Settings and Preferences**: Your AI model selections, formatting preferences, and app configuration settings
- **Journal Entries**: The journal entries you create and choose to save

### Automatically Collected Information
- **App Usage Data**: Basic information about how you use the app (feature usage, session duration)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect contacts or other personal files
- We do not track you across other apps or websites

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Processing
- All chat conversations are processed locally on your device and only shared with the AI provider of your choice
- Journal entries are created and stored locally unless you choose to export them
- App settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
- Log data is stored locally for debugging purposes

### Third-Party AI Services
When you provide API keys and use AI features, your chat messages are sent to your chosen AI provider:
- **OpenAI**: For GPT models (GPT-4o, GPT-4, GPT-3.5 Turbo)
- **Google Gemini**: For Gemini models (Gemini 1.5 Pro, Gemini 1.5 Flash)
- **Anthropic Claude**: For Claude models (Claude 3.5, Claude 3)

**Important**: Your messages are sent directly from your device to these AI services using your own API keys. We do not have access to, store, or process these conversations on our servers.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All user data is stored locally on your device
- API keys are securely stored using platform-native encrypted storage
- Chat conversations are temporarily stored in memory and not persisted unless you create a journal entry
- Journal entries are saved as local files only when you explicitly choose to export them

### Data Security
- API keys are encrypted using platform-native security mechanisms
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- All communication with AI services uses HTTPS encryption
- You have full control over data export and deletion

### Data Retention
- Chat conversations are not permanently stored unless you create and save a journal entry
- Saved journal entries remain on your device until you delete them
- App logs are automatically cleared based on system settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Do Not Share Your Data
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information
- We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do not have access to your conversations or journal entries

### Third-Party AI Services
- Your chat messages are sent directly to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Google, or Anthropic)
- These services have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data
- Please review the privacy policies of your chosen AI providers:
  - [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)
  - [Google AI Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, but since we don't collect or store your personal data on our servers, there is typically no information to disclose.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Control
- **Access**: All your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible to you at any time
- **Deletion**: You can delete journal entries, clear app data, or uninstall the app to remove all data
- **Export**: You can export your journal entries as Markdown files
- **API Key Management**: You can update or remove your AI API keys at any time

### App Permissions
The app requests platform-appropriate permissions:
- **Internet**: Required to communicate with AI services
- **Storage**: Required to save and export journal entries to files
- **Microphone** (optional): Required for speech-to-text functionality

You can revoke these permissions through your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

## Children's Privacy

Thought Smith is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

- Providing notice within the app if changes are material

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
